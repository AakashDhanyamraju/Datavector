Our Founder CEO’s Note

Sreekrishna started off his career as an e-commerce technology specialist two decades ago. He went on to become CMD of one of the leading wireless telecom product companies in India – Pointred Telecom Ltd., clocking revenues & order pipelines of more than US $100 Million under his leadership. He was instrumental in creating a wireless product R&D roadmap for the company that resulted in the company acquiring the largest order of WiMAX roll out in India. He also led Computer Access Pvt. Ltd. - a System Integrator and IT services company. One of the key projects that Sreekrishna directly handled was rolling out IT infrastructure for 2085 Schools for a State Government in India. This project was a 5 year Build-operate-Transfer model project valued at US $15 Million. Sreekrishna is currently a CEO & Co-Founder of DataVector Tech Pvt. Ltd., which is a new age cloud computing company having expertise in DevOps, AI & ML. Sreekrishna is an Engineer in Computer Science from PICT, Pune and Exec. Masters in International Business from IIFT, New Delhi.
