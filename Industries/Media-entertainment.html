Full-scale Media and Entertainment Software Development

Media and Entertainment Software Development Services


We provide full-scale media and entertainment software development services with our two-decade experience in the technology industry. Our teams have profound technical knowledge & are well-versed with the latest industry trends to support you in keeping your brand in the top recall.

Cloud-Based Application Development

Our custom cloud app development services help you keep up with customer expectations & accelerate time-to-market. We offer custom application development, migration of applications and workloads to virtualized environments, and rearchitecting them to enable you efficiently utilize cloud-native services.


Application Modernization

Our software product modernization practice covers application landscape assessment, architecting and implementation. We support you with platform/technology upgrade & migration, application enhancements, custom solution implementation, application integration, data transformation & cloudification.


Product Development

We cover the entire software product engineering services life cycle to support you across phases, starting from the idea being conceived to the deployment and user acceptance testing phase. It also includes product ideation, product architecture, product design, product testing & product migration and porting. Our team supports your product development initiatives, right from product ideation, through designing, development, and testing to release and maintenance.


Data Analytics and Visualization

We offer cloud-based business intelligence, data visualization & data engineering services as part of our Data Analytics Services focus. Our developers have hands-on experience on popular open-source technologies like Apache Hadoop, Spark, Python & Kafka. We can help you set up all the required elements of a digital data ecosystem – from the data lake, data warehouses, and OLAP (online analytical processing) to reporting, dashboarding & more.


API Development and Integration

We provide web, mobile & cloud API development, custom API development (JSON API, REST API development) and third-party API integration (with XML API). Our development team can support you with developing a comprehensive suite of microservices for faster development, updates, fixes, and deployment of services that can be changed independently.
